III The Target 目标

Upper Level SSAT, October 2027. Upper Level SSAT，2027 年 10 月。

The Upper Level test serves grades 8–11. Same structure as Middle Level, harder content, a larger score scale, and a percentile pool measured against the most competitive applicants in North America. Upper Level（高级）面向 8–11 年级学生。题目结构与中级一致，但内容更难、量程更大；百分位是和北美最有竞争力的私校申请群体进行比较。

A note on percentiles. SSAT percentiles compare each test-taker against same-grade, same-gender peers over a rolling three-year norm group. IV The Plan 三阶段规划

Foundation. Build. Sprint. 打基础。搭框架。冲刺。

Mr. Zhang's strategy — reading first, vocabulary patient and steady, math layered in once the school curriculum has caught up. Seventeen months. Three phases. 张老师的总体路线——阅读先行，词汇细水长流，等学校数学课进度跟上后再加入针对性的数学课。十七个月，分三阶段。

VI By Subject 分科目

Four lanes, one road. 四条赛道，同一条路。

Each section of the SSAT trains a different skill. The plan below shows how each is approached over the next seventeen months — what to learn, in what order, and how progress is measured. SSAT 的每一个科目训练一种不同的能力。下面按科目展开未来十七个月的安排——学什么、按什么顺序、用什么衡量进度。

R
Reading · 40 q in 40 min 阅读 · 40 题 / 40 分钟

Reading The lead subject. Where the plan begins. 阅读 头号科目。整个规划从这里开始。

Phase I lead第一阶段主科 7 passages7 篇文章 fiction · poetry · science · argument小说·诗歌·科普·论说

Both girls read well by ear today — but the test rewards method. Mr. Zhang structures Phase I around three gaps to fill, in this order: 两个孩子目前主要靠语感读，但考试更奖励"方法"。张老师把第一阶段的阅读训练分成三个递进任务：

  1. Terminology术语补齐 Simile, metaphor, personification, irony, tone, theme, inference — name the device, name the move. 比喻、隐喻、拟人、反讽、语气、主旨、推断——给修辞和阅读动作"命名"。
  2. Genre awareness题材熟悉度 Recognize each passage type's conventions: how to crack a fiction excerpt, what a persuasive essay is asking for, how a science passage signals its main claim. 熟悉每类文体的规律：如何快速拆解一段小说选段，论说文在问什么，科普类如何标记主旨。
  3. Question types题型识别 Detail · main idea · inference · vocabulary-in-context · tone · purpose · structure. Each has its own attack. 细节题、主旨题、推断题、语境词义题、语气题、目的题、结构题——每种题型都有专属解法。
V
Verbal · 60 q in 30 min 词汇 · 60 题 / 30 分钟

Vocabulary The slow burn. Trust the cumulative effect. 词汇 慢工出细活。靠累积效应。

30 synonyms + 30 analogies30 同义词 + 30 类比 ~500–1000 high-frequency words~500–1000 高频词 SAT-adjacent difficulty接近 SAT 难度

Neither girl has formally memorized SSAT vocabulary yet — Zoe especially is starting from feel only. The danger is overwhelm. The strategy is patience. 两个孩子都还没系统背过 SSAT 单词，Zoe 尤其是从语感出发。最大的风险是"压力过大、想放弃"，所以策略是——慢下来。

  1. Build the habit before the volume先建立背词习惯 Start with Middle Level lists; they nest inside Upper Level, so nothing is wasted. 15 new + 30 cumulative, daily. 从中级词表入手——中级词在高级词中是子集，不会白背。每日 15 新词 + 30 累计复习。
  2. Roots and affixes词根词缀 A small set of high-yield Latin and Greek roots unlocks dozens of words each. nov- means new; somn- means sleep; chrono- means time. 少量高产词根能解锁大量单词。nov- 表 "new"，somn- 表 "sleep"，chrono- 表 "time"，等等。
  3. Analogy logic类比逻辑 The trickiest verbal genre. Train the abstract relationships: degree, opposition, function, part-to-whole, cause-and-effect. 最难拿分的题型。系统训练抽象关系：程度、相反、功能、部分整体、因果。

VIII About the Test 关于考试

Eight parts, 167 questions. 八个部分，167 道题。

The Upper Level SSAT is identical in structure to the Middle Level, with harder content and a higher score ceiling. Six sections plus two breaks, roughly three hours start to finish. Upper Level（高级）与 Middle Level（中级）结构完全一致，区别在于内容难度和量程上限。共六段加两次休息，全长约 3 小时。

Scoring at a glance 计分要点

Raw score = correct − 0.25 × wrong. Blanks score 0. Raw is converted to a scaled score equated across test forms, then to a percentile against the same-grade, same-gender norm group from the past three years. 原始分 = 答对数 − 0.25 × 答错数；空题不扣分。原始分通过题目难度调整成"标准分"，再换算为相对同年级、同性别考生（过去三年）的百分位。

IX Format Options 考试形式

Four ways to take the same test. 四种方式，同一份考试。

X Roles 分工

For the parents · for the girls. 父母这边 · 孩子那边。

Long-arc test prep isn't a thing one person does. It's a household routine, and the routine works when each role is clear. Two short checklists below — one for parents, one for the students themselves. 长线备考从来不是一个人的事——是一个家庭的节奏。这套节奏能否成立，关键是分工清楚。下面两张清单，一张给父母，一张给孩子自己。

For Parents 父母 · 操作清单

  • Protect the weekly reading class保护每周阅读课时间 Don't schedule over it. Make it the same hour every week. Consistency beats intensity.不要安排其他活动撞这个时间。每周固定钟点上课，稳定比强度更重要。
  • Keep the daily habit at 15–30 minutes每日习惯控制在 15–30 分钟 Resist the urge to add more right away. Burnout in May 2026 means a worse score in October 2027.忍住"再加一点"的冲动。2026 年 5 月就累垮，意味着 2027 年 10 月分数更差。
  • Open the SSAT account by August 1, 20262026 年 8 月 1 日前注册 SSAT 账号 Practice the registration flow with the 2026–27 calendar before the real one matters.先用 2026–27 年度日历熟悉报名流程，到正考时不慌。
  • Track scores in one place分数统一记录在一个地方 Every mock: section scaled, raw, percentile (when available), and a one-line "felt like" note from the student.每次模考记录：单科标准分、原始分、百分位（如有），以及孩子的一句"考完感觉"。
  • Mark Aug 1, 2027 in red红笔标出 2027 年 8 月 1 日 Register both October and December dates the day they open. Don't wait a week.10 月和 12 月两个考试日要在开放报名当天就锁住，不要拖。
  • Talk about the why, not just the what谈"为什么"，而不只是"什么" School lists, school visits, school stories. The test is a step toward something specific — make that something visible.学校名单、参观、校友故事——考试是为了一个具体的方向，让这个方向"看得见"。

For Jasmine + Zoe 茉茉 + Zoe · 你们的清单

  • Read for fun, every day每天读自己喜欢的书 Twenty minutes of a book you love beats twenty minutes of a passage you don't. Both make you better at reading.读自己喜欢的书 20 分钟，远胜过被迫读 20 分钟练习题——但都会让你阅读变强。
  • Keep an error log you actually look at认真维护一本错题本 For every wrong answer: what kind of wrong was it — knowledge, careless, time, or trap? Re-read the log weekly.每道错题写明"错在哪一种"——知识、粗心、时间、陷阱。每周翻一遍。
  • Speak the vocabulary aloud把单词大声念出来 Words you've only seen on a page are easier to forget. Words you've said in a sentence are easier to keep.只看不读的单词容易忘——念出来、用一句话造个句，就记得更牢。
  • Don't grade your own mocks until the next day自己做的模考第二天再对答案 Distance lets you see your own mistakes more honestly. And calmer.隔一天再对答案，看自己错的时候更冷静、更诚实。
  • Skip questions you don't know完全不会的题就跳过 Wrong answers cost 0.25 each. Blanks cost zero. Only guess when you can rule out at least one option.答错扣 0.25，空着不扣。只在能排除至少一个选项时才猜。
  • Take Sundays off周日完全休息 Sleep, friends, anything that isn't prep. You'll come back sharper Monday. Trust this.睡觉、约朋友、不做题——周一你会更清醒，相信这个。
